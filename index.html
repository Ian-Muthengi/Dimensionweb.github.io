Intro

As a data scientist, my work primarily involves a multifaceted approach to managing and making sense of large datasets to drive strategic decision-making. This comprehensive process encompasses several key stages:
- Gathering raw data from various sources, which can include databases, APIs, CSV files, and real-time streaming data.
- Cleaning and transforming raw data into a usable format.
- Conducting exploratory data analysis (EDA) to uncover initial insights and identify patterns.
- Drawing actionable insights from the analysis to inform decision-making.
